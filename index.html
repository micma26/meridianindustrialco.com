About

Closing the gap between global cable manufacturers and Africa's infrastructure builders.

Meridian Industrial Co. is a specialist cable distributor serving Africa's energy, mining, and infrastructure sectors. We source IEC-certified industrial cable direct from Tier-1 manufacturers and supply it to EPC contractors, electrical installers, and project developers across Morocco and the wider continent.

Our Casablanca operating base and direct factory access deliver materially shorter lead times and more competitive pricing than the European import alternative — without compromising on specification, certification, or technical support.

What We Supply
01
Armoured Power Cable
LV and MV steel-wire armoured cable for power distribution across mining, industrial, and infrastructure environments. Available in single and multicore configurations.
02
Instrumentation & Control Cable
Screened and unscreened instrumentation cable for process control, measurement, and sensor applications. Armoured variants available for outdoor and buried installations.
03
LSZH Cable
Low smoke zero halogen cable for enclosed environments, process plant buildings, and tunnels where fire safety specification is mandatory.
04
Data & Comms Cable
Industrial-grade data and communications cable for telecoms infrastructure, site networks, and fibre roll-out projects across Morocco and the region.
